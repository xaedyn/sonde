Where does the heatmap live?

The heatmap shows the FULL history of every measurement as a dense color grid. It's the most space-hungry new feature. Where should it go?

A

Inside each lane — below the SVG chart

Each lane card grows taller to include a thin heatmap strip (20-30px) beneath the scatter chart. The heatmap scrolls with the lane. Keeps everything per-endpoint self-contained.

Shared strip between lanes and x-axis

A horizontal band below all lanes showing stacked heatmap rows (one per endpoint). Like a minimap of the full run. The x-axis moves below this strip.

C

Replace the left panel stats when enough data

Once stats are populated (after ~20 rounds), the left panel transitions from the stats grid to a per-endpoint heatmap grid. Stats move to hover tooltip or a secondary view. The heatmap becomes the lane's "identity" — you see the full history at a glance.
